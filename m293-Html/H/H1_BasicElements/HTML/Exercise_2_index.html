Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 300g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 400g Canned Tomatoes
  • Salt, Pepper, Oregano

Instructions

Finely chop the onion and garlic and fry them in a pan. Add the ground beef and fry until it is brown and crumbly. Add the tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Let everything simmer for 15 minutes.

In the meantime, cook the spaghetti in a pot of boiling salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and serve with the sauce.
